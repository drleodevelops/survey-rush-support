Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Survey Rush respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

Information the App Uses

Survey Rush may use receipt scan data, QR scan data, and survey-related information entered or scanned by the user in order to help open surveys and save validation codes.

Camera Access

The app requests access to your device camera only to scan receipts and QR codes. Camera data is used only for those features.

Saved Data

Survey Rush stores saved validation codes and related survey information on your device so you can view and manage them later.

Advertising

Survey Rush uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data as described in Google’s own privacy materials.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google AdMob and related consent tools in order to provide advertising and privacy choices.
